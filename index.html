Data source: api.weather.gov — NOAA's public weather API (observations endpoint: /stations/AV906/observations).
Station: KE8GJZ-2 (Gobles, MI) — amateur radio station reporting into the Citizen Weather Observer Program (CWOP) and relayed to NWS Grand Rapids (GRR).
Units: Observations are retrieved in metric (°C, km/h, Pa) and converted to US units (°F, mph, inHg) in the browser.
Coverage: Up to the 500 most-recent observations (~42 hours at 5-min cadence).
Disclaimer: Unofficial hobbyist weather page. Always consult weather.gov/grr for official forecasts and warnings.
Update cadence: Auto-refresh every 5 minutes.
Install: On Chrome (Android/desktop) or Safari (iOS), use the browser menu → "Add to Home Screen" / "Install" to use this page as a standalone app.
